Foodwriter, scientist and author

Meneer Wateetons has been a foodwriter, scientist, author of 9 famous cookbooks and leader of hundreds of humorous cooking workshops for 15 years. A man of many abilities which he presents on his websites. Available for business events, workshops or 'wild' weekends in Sweden. Within the website it is possible to view and order all of this, search an event in the schedule or buy one of his latest books.

About_

A WordPress application capable of many. Including a shop, books, events, blogs, workshops, recipes and smoothly handling tens of thousands orders.

Fully custom-made from scratch using Sage as a framework including a composer-based setup and a MVC structure with Laravel-Blade as template engine.
